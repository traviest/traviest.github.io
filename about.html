I've had a pretty boring life

The name is Travis. Born in eastern Tennessee, moved to California with my mother at 11. After that, traveling became a must. I contribute my mindset, morals, goals, and ambition to being fortunate enough to been exposed to such diverse places.

I attended elementary schools, middle-schools, and high schools in both California and Tennessee. After the initial move I bounced between living in Southern Cali with my mother and with my father in the mountains of Tennesee. I graduated from Daniel Boone High School in my hometown of TN. I then progressed to a regional state college, East Tennessee State University, where I am currently a senior in the Information Technology program.

After my final year at ETSU, I plan on continuing my education in pursuit of a graduate degree in computer science. My dream career revolves around web-design but I enjoy numerous aspects of the realm.

I have a weakness for passion and love and find myself constantly attached to those around me.
